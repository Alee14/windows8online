About

What is this ?

This is an online Windows 8 simulator. Fully made out of HTML, CSS and JS.

Why was this created ?

Many people don't know what "Metro UI" is. They are hesitating to download the Windows 8 Betas available. We would like to give those people an experience of Metro in a browser.
